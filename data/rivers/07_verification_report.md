# Verification Report: Cross-Border Rivers

## Method
6 well-known rivers spot-checked against their official River Basin Organization websites.

## Results

| River | Our Data | Primary Source | Status | Issue |
|-------|----------|---------------|--------|-------|
| Nile (11 countries) | Burundi, DR Congo, Egypt, Eritrea, Ethiopia, Kenya, Rwanda, South Sudan, Sudan, Tanzania, Uganda | NBI (nilebasin.org): same 11, but Eritrea is observer not member | MINOR | Eritrea is geographically correct but has observer status in NBI |
| Amazon (7 countries) | Bolivia, Brazil, Colombia, Ecuador, Guyana, Peru, Venezuela | OTCA (otca.org): 8 countries — includes **Suriname** | **FIX NEEDED** | **Suriname missing from our data** |
| Danube (19 countries) | Albania, Austria, BiH, Bulgaria, Croatia, Czech Republic, Germany, Hungary, Italy, Moldova, Montenegro, N. Macedonia, Poland, Romania, Serbia, Slovakia, Slovenia, Switzerland, Ukraine | ICPDR (icpdr.org): same 19 | MATCH | — |
| Mekong (6 countries) | Cambodia, China, Laos, Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam | MRC (mrcmekong.org): same 6 (China & Myanmar are dialogue partners) | MATCH | — |
| Rhine (9 countries) | Austria, Belgium, France, Germany, Italy, Liechtenstein, Luxembourg, Netherlands, Switzerland | ICPR (iksr.org): same 9 | MATCH | Belgium is specifically Wallonia; Italy's share is ~70 km² |
| Zambezi (8 countries) | Angola, Botswana, Malawi, Mozambique, Namibia, Tanzania, Zambia, Zimbabwe | ZAMCOM (zambezicommission.org): same 8 | MATCH | — |

## Summary
- **6 rivers verified**
- **4 exact matches** (Danube, Mekong, Rhine, Zambezi)
- **1 minor note** (Nile — Eritrea observer status, but geographically correct)
- **1 correction needed** (Amazon — Suriname missing)
- **Accuracy: 5/6 correct = 83%** (or 98%+ at country level — 1 country missing out of 61 checked)

## Correction Applied
- Amazon: added Suriname to country list
